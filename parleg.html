July 27th, 2023

Parle, owned by Parle Products, remains India's top FMCG brand, as per Kantar Worldpanels annual ranking. Seven of the top ten brands belong to domestic companies, and Parle maintains the top spot with 7449 million CRP (consumer reach points).

Parle-G

Parle-G is a brand of biscuits manufactured by Parle Products in India. A 2011 Nielsen survey reported that it is the best-selling brand of biscuits in the world. Parle-G is one of the oldest brand names in India, Parle-G is primarily eaten as a tea-time snack. For decades, the product was instantly recognized by its iconic white and yellow wax paper wrapper. The wrapper features a young girl (an illustration by Everest creative Maganlal Daiya back in the 1960s).


Did you know ?
Parle-G biscuits were earlier called 'Parle Gluco' Biscuits until the 1980s. The "G" in the name Parle-G originally stood for "Glucose", though a later brand slogan also stated "G for Genius".
